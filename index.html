An evangelical church in Ipswich

Ipswich Christian Fellowship is a small fellowship of believers who meet in the Name of the Lord Jesus in Ipswich. We come from a wide variety of backgrounds and are united by a common salvation in Jesus Christ alone.

A commitment to the Bible

We believe in the inerrancy, infallibility and sufficiency of the Bible as the inspired word of God and seek through it to know, love and obey Jesus Christ more fully, and to share the gospel of the Lord Jesus in Ipswich and beyond.

A warm welcome

Because of the Coronavirus pandemic we currently meet for Christian worship, communion and biblical teaching on Sundays at 3:00pm in Chantry Park in Ipswich and have an online prayer meeting and Bible study every Friday at 7:30pm. All our services are streamed online via Zoom.

If you would like to know us all at Ipswich Christian Fellowship better, we would love to welcome you and we invite you to come along and have fellowship with us. You can send us an email or contact us on our Facebook page.
